Welcome to Neuville Kaffee

Tucked between the majestic Galunggung and Sawal Mountains in Tasikmalaya, Neuville Indonesia stands as a beacon of culinary artistry and Sundanese charm. Established in February 2023, our café blends the essence of Sundanese culture with modern gastronomic experiences, offering visitors a unique blend of taste and tradition.

Be it the warmth of our artisanal coffee as the morning mist rolls off the mountains, the calming embrace of our selected teas after a day's exploration, or our delectable food and ice creams echoing the rich flavors of the region, Neuville promises a tantalizing journey for every taste bud.

Whether you’re soaking in the panoramic mountain views, delving into the rich history of Sundanese culture, or simply indulging in a gastronomic journey, Neuville Indonesia offers a multi-layered experience that beckons time and again.
